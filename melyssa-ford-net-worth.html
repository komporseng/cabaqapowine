Melyssa Ford Net Worth

Melyssa Ford is a Canadian actress and model with a net worth of $2 million. She was born in Toronto in 1976 and was discovered while working as a bartender in her twenties. Director Little X cast her in music videos, including Jay-Z’s “Big Pimpin'” and Alicia Keys’ “You Don’t Know My Name”. Ford has appeared in magazines like Maxim and XXL, and on TV shows like Soul Food and The Game. She has also acted in movies such as Think Like a Man and Think Like a Man Too. Ford currently stars on Bravo’s Blood, Sweat & Heels and earned the nickname “Jessica Rabbit” for her figure.
