Savannah Chrisley Looks Gorgeous in a Sultry Black and White Image She Shared on Instagram

Publish date: 2024-09-14

"Chrisley Knows Best" star Savannah Chrisley sported a sultry look and showed off her gorgeous legs in a recently shared black and white Instagram snapshot.

She was also pictured barefoot while stylishly standing on a balcony. With her hands firmly holding the shirt, Chrisley struck a sultry pose for the photo, which she captioned, "If looks could kill."

Savannah accessorized her outfit with a dainty necklace and a pair of diamond earrings that added a little sparkle to her look.

"You look absolutely gorgeous."

A second follower commented: "In this picture right here, you could play Catwoman," while another third fan sweetly added, "You look so beautiful."

Despite calling off their wedding, Chrisley and her boyfriend appear more in love than ever.

Taking to Instagram, Kerdiles shared a video of himself with Chrisley at a concert. She was seen happily singing to him before kissing him.

In his caption, Kerdiles wished Chrisley a happy birthday and described her as the woman who changed his life. He concluded the sweet note by writing:

"So proud of the woman you have become and continue to be. Love you, kid!"

Kerdiles and Chrisley got engaged in December 2018 and were expected to tie the knot this past May. But the wedding never took place.

In June, Chrisley opened up on why she and Kerdiles decided to postpone their wedding. She reportedly explained that they realized things went too fast in their relationship.

Chrisley added that they both decided to go back to dating. According to her, it is okay to go against stereotypes about being engaged and getting married.

While it remains unclear what prompted the couple to call off their wedding, Chrisley once clarified that it had nothing to do with infidelity. According to her, they are both figuring things out and are trying to experience growth as individuals and in their respective careers.

Born on August 11, 1997, Savannah Chrisley is an actress best known for her roles in "Sharknado 4: The 4th Awakens", and the reality show, "Chrisley Knows Best."
