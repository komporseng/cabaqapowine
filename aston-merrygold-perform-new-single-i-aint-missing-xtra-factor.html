Aston Merrygold To Perform New Single 'I Ain't Missing You' On Xtra Factor

Publish date: 2024-04-26

It’s been a while since former JLS star Aston Merrygold launched his solo career with debut single ‘Get Stupid’, but now the pop cutie is back with his brilliant new single ‘I Ain’t Missing You’.

Merrygold performed the track at Nickelodeon’s Slimefest in Blackpool yesterday, and the crowd loved it.

But tonight the RnB crooner will make his return to live TV with a performance of the single, when he performs the track on the Xtra Factor .

Aston confirmed the news earlier today via Instagram and Twitter;

Despite performing the song on tour during his support slot for US superstar Jason Derulo, the 28-year-old has yet to announce a release date for his second solo single! But judging by the timing of his live TV performance fans shouldn’t have long to wait until they can download ‘I Ain’t Missing You’.

Not only has Merrygold been performing the single he has also been busy filming the official music video. He has been sharing exclusive shots of the process with his 176k Instagram followers.

You can catch Aston Merrygold performing his latest single ‘I Ain’t Missing You’ on Xtra Factor tonight at 9pm on ITV2.
