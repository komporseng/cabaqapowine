Publish date: 2024-09-15

Gerard Piqué, the Spanish professional soccer player, has been a prominent figure in the sports and entertainment industry. His romantic relationship with Colombian singer Shakira has been a subject of public interest.

Despite their long-term partnership and two children together, the couple never officially tied the knot. Let's delve into the details of Gerard Piqué's marital status and his relationship with Shakira.

Relationship with Shakira

Gerard Piqué and Shakira's relationship began in the early 2010s, and they share two children, sons Milan and Sasha. The couple's split was announced in 2022 after an 11-year partnership.

Despite their long-term commitment, it's important to note that Gerard Piqué and Shakira were never legally married. This distinction is crucial when discussing Piqué's marital status.

Marriage Status

It's clear from various sources that Gerard Piqué and Shakira never formalized their partnership through marriage. Although the pair shared a deep bond and a family, they did not enter into a legally binding marital union.

Shakira herself addressed this when asked about marriage in an interview, stating, "We already have what’s essential, you know? We have a union, a love for each other, and a baby. I think that those aspects of our relationship are already established, and marriage is not going to change them".

The Split

The public announcement of their split in 2022 sparked significant interest in the media. Shakira herself has been open about the challenges she faced following the breakup.

In a new interview with Marie Claire UK, she expressed her thoughts on love and relationships, indicating that while she always believed that having a partner was the most important thing in her life, she has learned to move on from the unexpected turns in her personal life.

Impact on Shakira's Career

Shakira's personal life, particularly her relationship with Gerard Piqué, has had a notable impact on her career. In a recent appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, Shakira revealed that her former relationship with Piqué had hindered her from producing a full album, and she attributed the seven-year gap between her latest collection and her previous album to the challenges she faced due to her relationship. 

Shakira's New Album

Shakira's new album, Las Mujeres Ya No Lloran (Women No Longer Cry) , is a reflection of the pain and transformation she experienced during the difficult times in her personal life.

The album includes tracks that convey her emotions and experiences, showcasing her journey from pain to creativity and resilience.

 

While Gerard Piqué and Shakira shared a deeply committed and long-lasting relationship, they were never married. The announcement of their split in 2022 marked the end of their romantic partnership.

It's evident that their separation has had a significant impact on both of their lives, as reflected in Shakira's career and her recent album release. Despite the challenges they faced, they remain devoted co-parents to their two children.
