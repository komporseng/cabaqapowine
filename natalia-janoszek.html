Wiki/Biography

She was born on Friday, 15 June 1990 (age 29 years; as in 2019), in Bielsko-Biala, Poland.  After school, she did an International Business Program from the University of Warsaw.

Career

Since the age of three, she was passionate about singing and dancing. Later, at the age of 16, she participated in the Miss Teen Poland competition. She represented her country at the international level in Miss Peace International in Cyprus (2009). She has won more than 20 beauty pageants at national and international levels.

Natalia Janoszek Posing with Her Beauty Pageant Award

When she was participating in the Supermodel International Contest in 2012, one of the renowned Bollywood producers noticed her and approached her for his next movie. In 2013, she appeared in the Bollywood movie ‘Dreamz: The Movie.’ She played the character of ‘Aafreen’ in the movie.

A Still From Natalia Janoszek- Dreamz The Movie

In 2014, she appeared in the movie ‘Flame: An Untold Love Story.’ Later, in 2016, she was seen in the Hollywood movie ‘The Green Fairy.’

Natalia Janoszek in the Hollywood Movie

In 2019, she appeared in the Bollywood movie ‘Chicken Curry Law,’ along with the renowned actors Ashutosh Rana and Makarand Deshpande. She also did an item song ‘Mera Sufi Ishq’ in this movie.

  • Tropic Beauty (Las Vegas, USA), 2013 for the Best Dress
  • JIFFA (India), 2018 for the Best International Actress
