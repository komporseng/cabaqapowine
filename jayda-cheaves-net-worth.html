Many people are wondering how Jayda Cheaves got rich and famous.

Cheaves is an entrepreneur with various revenue streams.

She has been hustling since the age of sixteen and started out by selling clothes on Poshmark, which provided her with retail experience and inspired her to develop a successful business of her own.

After realizing the potential of e-commerce and recognizing that she could earn more money by selling clothes independent of Poshmark, Jayda launched her own platform and was soon turning a healthy profit without having to surrender significant amounts of money in seller fees. 

And given her growth mindset, Jayda wasn’t content with the fact that she was running a successful e-commerce site.

She had her sights set on a brick-and-mortar retail opportunity, which led her to set up her first shop – Amour Jayda – in Atlanta, Georgia.

While clothing still formed the bulk of Jayda’s retail offer, she diversified and started selling various hair care products, cosmetics, wigs, and other women’s fashion items. 

Thanks to the success of Amor Jayda, she was inspired to diversify even further, which led her to open her online hair Wayda Shop.

Since starting out as an entrepreneur in her teen years, Jayda has enjoyed enormous success in her retail endeavors, which is the reason that she boasts such a healthy bank balance today. 

And due to the success of her e-commerce businesses, Jayda has developed professional partnerships with the likes of Fashion Nova, GSU WOO, and Pretty Little Thing.

In collaboration with these brands, Jayda has released her own clothing lines and cosmetic products, enabling her to branch out into new markets and appeal to a broader customer base.
